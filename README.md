# samoleary

This repository contain a synopsis of several technical projects I have undertaken in classes at Middlebbury College, independent projects, and internships. 

The R files titled final_functions, final_workflow, and multivariable_stats are associated with a independent project titled: Revising the Megalopolis: Empirical Methods for Defining Megapolitan Regions that was presented at the American Association of Geographers Annual Meeting in San Francisco in March 2026. These files consist of my spatial analysis workflow and statistical analysis that I used to achieve my results, which found that prior megapolitan delineations were flawed, and that there could be a 'sweet spot' for urban agglomerations between 2-5 million people where standards of living were in fact higher than the larger agglomerations (5 Million+)
